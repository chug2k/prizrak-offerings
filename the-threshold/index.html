The Threshold

Three acts before sleep. Set something down. Hold something lightly. Leave one window open. Then cross.

01 / Set down
Something heavy from today that you're consciously releasing until morning. Not denying it — setting it on the stone outside the door.
02 / Hold lightly
Something that matters but doesn't need to be solved tonight. You're not forgetting it. You're just not strangling it.
03 / Open window
Not a task. A genuine curiosity. Small and real. Something that makes tomorrow slightly interesting rather than just survivable.
