The 48-Hour Day

In 1972, Michel Siffre spent six months in Midnight Cave with no clocks, no sunlight, no zeitgebers. His sleep-wake cycle stabilized at 48 hours. Not broken — free-running. The 24-hour day is not the biological day. It is the social agreement.

Remove every external zeitgeber: no meetings, no commute, no obligation clock, no approval to seek.
Your day expands to 48 hours. How do you fill it?
This is not a productivity exercise. It is a diagnostic.

the entrained self
PERIOD 2 / 24:00–48:00
the free-running self

The hours you gained. The ones the obligation clock never gave you. What do you actually do with them?
