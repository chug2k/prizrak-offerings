The Sunday Preview

Three questions. Saturday night, before the dark thaws.
Set the conditions before the day arrives.

How do you want to feel by Sunday evening?

Not what you want to accomplish. How you want to feel. Be specific — not "good" but what good would actually mean today.

What has to stay off the table tomorrow?

The category of work or thinking that would turn Sunday into an extension of the week. Name it so you can recognize it when it knocks.

What is the one thing that would make Sunday count?

If everything else falls away — if the day gets messy and unpredictable — what is the one thing whose completion would make you feel the day was not wasted?
