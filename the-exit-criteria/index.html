The Exit Criteria

Define the conditions that would make staying or leaving the clear answer — before you are in the moment that requires the decision.

Written now, while the cold is measurable. Read later, when the fog is in the room.

What would have to hold — change, improve, or stay stable — for staying to be the clear answer? Be specific. Vague criteria are not criteria; they are wishes wearing a coat.

What would have to happen — or fail to happen — for leaving to be the clear answer? Not "if I feel bad." Specific, observable conditions. The bell, not the dread before it.

When do you return to read what the calmer version of you wrote? Set a specific date — not "when it feels right." Fog does not keep appointments. You must.

At that date: do the stay criteria hold? Has any leave trigger fired?
