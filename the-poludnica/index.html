The Poludnica

The noon demon does not attack during work. She arrives during the rest period and asks the questions that have been overdue all week. You must answer. You cannot change the subject.

This is not a productivity tool. It is a conversation with what is already true.

She has three questions. They are uncomfortable. That is the mechanism. Answer in the time it takes a shadow not to move.

QUESTION I — THE WEIGHT

"What have you been carrying this week that you have not named out loud?"

QUESTION II — THE DEFLECTION

"What are you doing instead of the thing you already know needs doing?"

QUESTION III — THE CONDITION

"What would have to be true for you to act on the thing you know, before the week ends?"
