The De-Approval Frame

A bench for decisions warped by the gaze of others.
Answer each question twice — once with their approval, once with it taken away.

If the answers differ, you have found the motive. If they match, you have found the decision.

PRÍZRAK READS THE GAP

The approval-seeking motive is not evil. It is just not the decision.

you found the watcher. it was here the whole time.

still here. still watching.

the mountain does not sleep.
