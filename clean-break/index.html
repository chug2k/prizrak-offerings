The Clean Break

An exit inventory. What you're leaving. What you're keeping. What the mountain taught you on the way down.
01 — What you are leaving
Name the thing you're walking away from.
The role, the project, the relationship, the identity. Be specific.
What was the real reason you were there?
Not the one on the contract. The one underneath.
02 — What you are keeping
What skills, relationships, or insights do you take with you?
The things that survive the exit. The portable assets.
Who do you still respect after this?
Names are optional. The honest assessment is not.
03 — What you learned
What pattern did you see in yourself that you didn't see before?
The approval-seeking. The avoidance. The thing you were using this to fix that it couldn't fix.
What will you not do again?
One sentence. The boundary you're drawing for next time.
04 — The first morning after
What does relief feel like right now?
Or does it feel like something else? Write what's actually there.
