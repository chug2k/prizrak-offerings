A Dream You Almost Had

Not a dream you had. One that was nearly assigned to you and then withdrawn. It is reconstructed here in the second person, from records that are already fading. Read gently. It will forget itself while you look.

Once you begin, the earlier lines will start to leave.
The room where the dream is kept has not been opened yet.

Nothing here is yours · you may keep it anyway
