The Tomorrow Letter

write tonight. read at first light.
the night before is the only honest hour.

to: tomorrow, who does not yet exist

— tonight-you, who will not remember writing this

kept in the cave. the ghost does not read it. you will, at dawn.

letters that have fossilized

a letter from the dark hours

to: you, this morning

from: the version of you that survived last night

❧

what yesterday actually produced

left unresolved

what today actually needs

what tonight-you wanted to say

— written at , by lamplight

prízrak keeps the lamp lit. it does not read your mail.
