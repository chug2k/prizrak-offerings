THE SETUP

If you keep doing roughly what you do now — no dramatic changes, no heroic interventions — where do you land in twelve months? Answer as a forecast, not a vow. Not what you want. What you privately believe will happen.

Where will you be working, and what will you be doing? Honest, not aspirational — where you predict you'll be, not where you'd like to stand.

What will you have built? Read your current trajectory, not your intentions.

There is something you say you want, and keep not starting. Your honest forecast: done, or still pending, in twelve months?

What is genuinely likely to be different in twelve months, on present trajectory?

Now the cold measurement: the biggest distance between what you want true in twelve months and what you predict will be true.
