The Resting Hypothesis

Not your plan. Your prediction.
What do you actually believe will be true in 12 months?

State it before it states itself through your decisions.

The setup

If you continue doing roughly what you are doing now — no dramatic changes, no heroic interventions — where will you be in 12 months? Answer as a prediction, not a goal. Not what you want. What you actually believe will happen.

Where will you be working, and what will you be doing? Be honest — not where you want to be, where you predict you'll be.

What will you have built or created? If you are being honest about your current trajectory, not aspirational.

There is something you say you want to do that you keep not doing. What is your prediction about whether you will have done it in 12 months?

What is actually likely to be different in 12 months, based on current trajectory?

Looking at the above: what is the biggest difference between what you want to be true in 12 months and what you predict will be true?
