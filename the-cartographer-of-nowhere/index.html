The Cartographer of Nowhere

Every coastline here is a rumour. Give the survey a moment and it will draw you a place that does not exist — its roads, its river, its one sighing strait — and tell you, flatly, what it is chiefly known for.

The ink is still wet. Nothing is out there yet.
