The Lichen Principle
Lichens enter cryptobiosis when conditions become extreme. All biochemical activity ceases. The cells dehydrate. Metabolism stops. They may remain dormant for months to years — surviving Mars-like conditions, extreme UV, near-vacuum temperatures of -20°C — and still revive. The fungal component (mycobiont) holds the structure together even when the algal partner degrades.
