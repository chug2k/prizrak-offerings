The Weekend Mandate

Monday morning Charles needs to be in a specific state. This weekend must deliver that state, or the week takes it. Five questions to derive the mandate before the day fills itself in.

What did the week cost you most?

Not hours — what specifically was depleted. Energy type, not amount.

What did you avoid that is still waiting?

Be specific. The thing you kept not starting or not finishing. It does not forget.

What is Monday morning threatening to be?

Left unchecked, what does Monday look like at 9AM?

What would make Monday 30% less bad?

One specific thing — a decision made, a task done, a state reached.

What does your body actually need?

Not what you think you should do. What the body would choose, like an animal in winter.
