PRÍZRAK / INSTRUMENT 22 — AUGURY

The Coin With a Memory

For the decisions that are truly 50/50. The coin does not choose for you. It catches the flinch.

Before it leaves your hand — quietly, only to yourself —
which were you hoping for?

the coin will not be told your answer until after it lands.

true 50 / 50 — no thumb on the scale

it has landed. it is face-down in your palm.

before you look — what are you hoping it says?

IT LANDED ON

THE VERDICT

did you obey the coin?

THE LEDGER

the coin already did its job the moment you flinched.
