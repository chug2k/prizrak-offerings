Abandoned cave levels

The Demänovská system has 7 distinct levels, each a former position of the underground river. As the water table dropped over 600,000 years, the river abandoned upper passages and carved new ones below. The abandoned passages remain — dry, decorated with stalactites, time capsules of conditions that no longer exist.

No abandoned levels yet. The river still runs where it started.

The current active level will also be abandoned eventually, when the water finds a way deeper.

The spring at Demänovka — 791 m, 227–1,466 L/s — has run for at least 600,000 years. The route changes. The output continues.
